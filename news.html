The Power of Consistency: The Journey Behind Tropicana Mall, Kano

On September 30, 2025, Tropicana Mall was officially commissioned, marking a significant milestone not only for McDream Concepts Ltd but for the people and economy of Kano State. NILE UNIVERSITY CAREER DAY

We happily joined the NILE University Career Day in June 2023, our team talked to students about real estate investment. We wanted to show students that real estate is a good way to invest money. We talked about how it can help with money in the long run and how it’s stable. But we didn’t only talk about investing, we told students they can also have jobs in real estate. There are many kinds of jobs like designing, marketing, and managing. Our team shared stories about their jobs and how exciting they are, we hope that by being at NILE University’s Career day we made students think about real estate. We want them to see the chances for good jobs and investing in the future. Thanks to NILE University for having us, and thanks to all the students who talked to us. We’ll keep making great homes and helping young people in real estate.
